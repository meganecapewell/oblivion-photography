Our Philosophy

Megan Elizabeth, the founder, believed that every photo can stand the test of time. A photo can bring back memories of a childhood, of spending a day with a loved one, or laughing over the silly memories you had with your friends.

Over time as she discovered how powerful a photo can be, her team grew to have a few employees that shared in the same philosophy.
